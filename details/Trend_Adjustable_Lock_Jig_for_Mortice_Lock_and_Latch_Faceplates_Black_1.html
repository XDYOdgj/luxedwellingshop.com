Trend Adjustable Lock Jig

The Trend Adjustable Lock Jig is a must-have tool for anyone who wants to install mortice locks and latches with confidence. Its adjustable sliding plates and stops make it easy to fit a wide range of faceplate sizes, including lengths from 1-3/16 inches up to 9-27/32 inches. This flexibility ensures that you can handle both standard and unusual sizes efficiently.

The engraved underside of the jig is designed for quick alignment with common door widths, helping you save time and avoid mistakes during installation. Whether you're doing a simple DIY project or a more complex commercial task, this tool provides consistent results. Its compatibility with 1/2-inch routers and 1-3/16 inch guide bush makes it an ideal fit for Trend’s popular cutters, ensuring smooth and precise fitting of multi-point lock strips in wooden doors.

Key Features

  • Adjustable sliding plates for mortice and latch faceplates up to 9-27/32 inches
  • Engraved underside for quick and accurate setup on common door widths
  • Adaptable stops for various guide bush diameters and straight cutters
  • Compatible with 1/2-inch router and 1-3/16 inch guide bush
  • Precision fitting for multi-point lock strips in wooden doors

Why Choose This Jig?

Whether you're working on new construction or retrofitting existing doors, the Trend Adjustable Lock Jig is the perfect accessory for efficient and precise mortice work. It allows for easy adjustment and secure positioning, making it easy to use for both professionals and casual do-it-yourselfers. Its robust build ensures durability and reliability for demanding environments, while its clever design helps you achieve professional-level results, no matter the project size.

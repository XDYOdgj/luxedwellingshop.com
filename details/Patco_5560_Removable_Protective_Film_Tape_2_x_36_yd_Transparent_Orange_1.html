Patco 5560 Removable Protective Tape

A versatile and reliable choice for protecting surfaces during installation, manufacturing, or any DIY project.

What Makes It Special?

Patco 5560 is designed to provide secure yet removable protection. Its moderate tack ensures it sticks firmly but won’t damage surfaces when pulled off. The acrylic adhesive is engineered to leave no residue, making it ideal for temporary use on various materials like glass, metal, and plastic.

With a durable polyethylene film backing, this tape can withstand abrasion and moisture, giving you long-lasting protection. It conforms easily to complex shapes and tight corners, offering flexibility that’s hard to match. The 500% elongation feature makes it perfect for wrapping awkward edges and surfaces with precision.

Whether you’re masking for painting, repairs, or assembly, this tape ensures your projects stay clean and undamaged. Its extreme temperature resistance, from -40°F to 190°F, makes it suitable for use in both freezing and hot environments, enhancing its versatility across different applications.

Where Can You Use It?

Perfect for both residential and commercial use, this tape is a must-have for any contractor or DIY enthusiast. It’s ideal for protecting countertops, shelves, and fixtures during remodeling or construction projects. From masking delicate surfaces to handling tough conditions, Patco 5560 delivers reliable performance and exceptional value.

Key Features
  • Secure yet removable protection with moderate tack
  • Acrylic adhesive leaves no sticky residue on surfaces
  • Resists abrasion, solvents, and moisture for long-lasting use
  • Conforms to tricky shapes and curved surfaces with ease
  • 500% elongation allows for precise and clean masking
Why Choose Patco 5560?

Patco 5560 combines practicality and performance in one product. Whether you're working on a large-scale project or a small repair, this tape ensures your work remains pristine and undisturbed. Its adaptability and reliability make it a top choice for professionals and homeowners alike.

Enjoy a clean, hassle-free masking experience with this residue-free tape. With a broad range of applications and excellent durability, it’s the perfect solution for all your protective needs.

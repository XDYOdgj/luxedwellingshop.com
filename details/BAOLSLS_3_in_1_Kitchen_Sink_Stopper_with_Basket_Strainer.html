Upgrade Your Kitchen with the Perfect 3 in 1 Drain Stopper

Crafted from high-quality brass and finished with electroplating, this 3 in 1 kitchen sink stopper offers a sleek, durable design that stands the test of time. Its advanced material ensures resistance to corrosion, keeping your sink looking great for years to come.

Designed for US standard 3-1/2 inch sink drains, this versatile stopper integrates seamlessly into both residential and commercial kitchens. Whether used in a home, restaurant, or even while camping, it adapts to your needs with ease.

One of the standout features is the removable ABS plastic basket strainer, which simplifies cleaning and helps trap food particles, reducing the risk of clogs. The pop-up bounce core design allows for a smooth and efficient switch between water storage and drainage modes, providing convenience every time you use your sink.

MILDPLUS 30'' X 36'' Extra Large Bed Pads with Adhesive Strips

Designed for ultimate comfort and reliability, MILDPLUS bed pads are crafted with a soft, breathable non-woven fabric that keeps your skin dry and refreshed all night long. Whether you're looking after an adult, senior, child, or pet, these underpads provide a versatile and effective solution for everyday use.

Each pad features two wide adhesive strips on the back, ensuring they stay securely in place without shifting, offering consistent support and protection. The high absorbency of these disposable underpads helps quickly soak up any liquid, maintaining a clean and hygienic environment.

With upgraded materials, MILDPLUS pads are more durable and less prone to tearing, making them a long-lasting choice for your needs. The non-compressed packaging keeps the pads separate and intact, so you can store, carry, and use them with ease.

Key Features

  • Soft and breathable non-woven fabric for comfortable sleep
  • Two wide adhesive strips for secure placement
  • High absorbency to manage liquids and protect surfaces
  • Durable construction with improved raw materials
  • Non-compressed packaging for easy storage and use

Why Choose MILDPLUS?

MILDPLUS bed pads are perfect for use on mattresses, sofas, or even in wheelchairs. They offer a leak-proof barrier that ensures your space stays clean and dry. The breathable surface enhances comfort, while the simple adhesive design makes them easy to apply without extra fastening.

This versatile product is ideal for caregivers, pet owners, and families. With 30 pads in each pack, you'll have enough to meet your daily requirements, making it an economical and reliable solution for quality bedding protection.

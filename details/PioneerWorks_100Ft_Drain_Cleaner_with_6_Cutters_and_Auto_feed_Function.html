PioneerWorks 100Ft Electric Drain Cleaner

This electric drain cleaner is built for speed and efficiency, helping you clear stubborn clogs in pipes from 1" to 4" in diameter. Perfect for both home and commercial plumbing projects, it's a reliable tool that every professional plumber should have in their kit.

The auto-feed function ensures smooth and continuous operation, making it ideal for tackling tough blockages without pause. Its built-in GFCI protection keeps you safe from electrical shocks, providing peace of mind during every job, whether it's a quick fix or a full-scale repair.

Versatile and Durable Design

This drain cleaner comes with six interchangeable cutters, including Drop Head Auger, Boring Bulb Cutter, C-Cutter, Side Cutter, Spade Cutter, and Saw Tooth Cutter. Each is designed to handle different types of blockages, ensuring you can solve a wide range of plumbing issues with one powerful tool.

Featuring a comfortable, adjustable handle and heavy-duty wheels, this machine is easy to maneuver through tight spaces, up stairs, or across uneven surfaces. Its 100-foot long steel core cable is reinforced for durability and long-lasting performance, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.

Key Features

  • Power motor for fast and efficient drain cleaning
  • Auto-feed function with flexible air-activated footswitch
  • Heavy-duty 14" wheels for easy mobility
  • 100Ft x 1/2" steel core cable for durability
  • 6 quick-change cutters for versatile unclogging

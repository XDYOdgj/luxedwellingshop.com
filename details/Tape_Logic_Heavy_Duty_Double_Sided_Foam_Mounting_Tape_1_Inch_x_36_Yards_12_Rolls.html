Heavy-Duty Double Sided Foam Mounting Tape

Tape Logic offers a reliable and high-performance double-sided foam mounting tape designed for a wide range of indoor applications. Whether you're working on a DIY project, organizing your workspace, or creating something unique, this tape provides a secure and clean bonding solution with exceptional durability and ease of use.

The flexible polyethylene foam backing ensures a firm grip on any surface, while the strong rubber adhesive offers enduring hold and repositionability. This makes it perfect for hanging decorations, joining materials, or temporary mounting needs without leaving residue or damaging surfaces.

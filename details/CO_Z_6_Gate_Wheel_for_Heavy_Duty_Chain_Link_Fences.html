CO-Z 6" Gate Wheel with 500 LB Capacity for Smooth and Reliable Operation

The CO-Z gate wheel is engineered to bring effortless movement to your chain link fence gate. Its heavy-duty construction is built to handle gates weighing up to 500 lb, providing long-lasting durability and strength. The adjustable angle feature allows for perfect gate clearance, ensuring your gate opens and closes smoothly without sagging. A corrosion-resistant zinc-plated bracket and rubber wheel make it ideal for use in all weather conditions, while the serrated design locks the wheel securely onto the tube. Whether you're maintaining a residential or commercial gate system, the CO-Z gate wheel is a simple, efficient, and reliable solution.

Perfect Fit for All Types of Metal Swing Gates and Chain Link Fences

Designed for undermount applications, the CO-Z gate wheel offers unmatched support for metal swing gates and chain link fence gates. Its corrosion-resistant zinc-plated bracket ensures long-term performance even in harsh weather, while the rubber wheel provides smooth, quiet operation. The adjustable angle and U-shaped bolt allow for a secure fit on tubes ranging from 1-5/8" to 2", making it versatile and adaptable to various gate designs. The included installation hardware simplifies setup, eliminating the need for additional purchases. A must-have for any gate system requiring reliable and trouble-free performance.

Precision Epoxy Gun for Perfect Panel Bonding

This epoxy gun is crafted for reliable and accurate application of 400 mL/13.5 fl oz of dual-component panel bonding adhesive. With a 1:1 mix ratio, it ensures even bonding strength for all your projects. The manual design eliminates the need for extra equipment, making it ideal for individual use or small-scale repairs.

Featuring a built-in pre-mix system, this tool streamlines the process of combining two-part adhesives directly from the cartridge. This means you save time and money while achieving perfect results every time. The heavy-duty metal components provide long-lasting durability, and the premium finish enhances both the look and feel of the gun.

Whether you're working on DIY fixes or professional applications, this versatile epoxy gun is your go-to solution for bonding panels, sealing seams, or repairing surfaces.

Transform Your Outdoor Spaces with Smart LED Deck Lighting

The Sumaote LED Recessed Deck Light Kit brings modern design and smart functionality to your home. Whether you're lighting up your deck, steps, or garden path, these lights are built to shine bright and last long, no matter the weather.

Effortless Smart Control

With WiFi & Bluetooth smart control, adjusting your lighting has never been easier. Simply connect via the 'FVTLED' app to manage brightness, color, flashing speed, and even set group functions for multiple light kits. Enjoy seamless hands-free experience by using voice commands with Alexa or Google Assistant.

Weather-Resistant Design

Rated IP67 waterproof, these deck lights are perfect for outdoor use in any climate. From rain to snow, they provide durable and worry-free illumination for your walkways, patios, and backyard areas.

Colorful and Customizable Lighting

Choose from 16 million color options to create the perfect ambiance for your outdoor living space. These LED deck lights offer vibrant, customizable lighting that adapts to your mood and decor.

Energy-Saving and Safe Design

The low-voltage DC12V system ensures energy efficiency without compromising on safety. Each light consumes minimal power and is built with SMD5050 LEDs for bright, even illumination across your property. Ideal for both residential and commercial use.

With a 20-pack setup, these deck lights are perfect for lighting up your steps, floors, and garden paths. Whether you're looking for practical lighting or a touch of style, Sumaote has the solution in a smart home-friendly package.

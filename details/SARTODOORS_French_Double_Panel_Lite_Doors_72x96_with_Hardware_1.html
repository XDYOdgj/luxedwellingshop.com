SARTODOORS French Double Panel Lite Doors

Elevate your space with these beautifully crafted French double panel lite doors. Designed for both style and performance, they are ideal for use in bathrooms and bedrooms, offering a sleek, modern look that fits seamlessly into your interior design.

Features

  • Featuring 2 glass paneled doors with frosted opaque glass and a sophisticated white silk finish
  • Constructed from sturdy solid MDF with an eco-veneer surface for a natural, long-lasting appearance
  • Equipped with non-mortise hinges and satin nickel levers/knobs for a refined finish and simplified installation

Key Benefits

These doors are not only durable but also built to last with scratch and stain-resistant properties. Their eco-friendly construction makes them a sustainable choice for modern homeowners.
